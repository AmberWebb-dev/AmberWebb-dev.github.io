Hello, I'm Amber!

I am a current student at Full Sail University working on my Bachelors In Video Game Development. I graduated from TideWater Community College with my associates in basic Computer Science. I am now working on becomming a Game Programmer and strive to learn how to create many different types of fun projects.

I would describe myself as passionate and hard working, I love learning new thing and solving difficult problems. I have been told by teachers and classmates that my code is neat and easy to read. I have often found myself losing track of time when I am trying to work out a solution to a difficult problem, I have a hard time letting it go until I have found the proper solution.

Aside from my work ethic, I am a friendly and outgoing person who tends to make friends wherever I go. In my free time I enjoy playing video games with my friends, watching anime, and spending time with my family.

If you want to know more, you can find my Contact Information and my Résumé in the tabs at the bottom of the home page. You can also find my Social Media in the Contact tab on the main page.
